In the US, auto policies have the following coverages Liability, either in split limits or combined single limits, bodily injury liability & property damage liability. Collision coverage pays to repair your car, regardless of fault due to an accident and lastly, comprehensive coverage repairs your car if it is not damaged from an accident.
